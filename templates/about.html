About TOS & PP Scraper

TOS & PP Scraper is a tool designed to automatically extract and summarize the Terms of Service (TOS) and Privacy Policy (PP) of websites.

By scraping these documents, we aim to help users quickly understand a website's terms without needing to read lengthy legal text.

How It Works:

  • Input a website domain (e.g., example.com).
  • Extracts the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links.
  • Summarizes both documents into concise 100-word and 10-word summaries.
  • Generates a word cloud of common terms used in the TOS and PP.

Why It Matters:

Understanding the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy of websites is essential for protecting user privacy and ensuring compliance with various legal agreements.

This tool is here to help you save time and make informed decisions by offering easy-to-understand summaries and insights.
